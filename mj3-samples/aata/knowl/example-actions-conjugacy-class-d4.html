Example 14.13.

The center of \(D_4\) is \(\{ (1), (13)(24) \}\text{,}\) and the conjugacy classes are

\begin{equation*} \{ (13), (24) \}, \quad \{ (1432), (1234) \}, \quad \{ (12)(34), (14)(23) \}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Thus, the class equation for \(D_4\) is \(8 = 2 + 2 + 2 + 2\text{.}\)
