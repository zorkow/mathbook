Let \(H\) be the subgroup of \(S_3\) defined by the permutations \(\{(1), (123), (132) \}\text{.}\) The left cosets of \(H\) are

\begin{gather*} (1)H = (1 2 3)H = (132)H = \{(1), (1 23), (132) \}\\ (1 2)H = (1 3)H = (2 3)H = \{ (1 2), (1 3), (2 3) \}\text{.} \end{gather*}

The right cosets of \(H\) are exactly the same as the left cosets:

\begin{gather*} H(1) = H(1 2 3) = H(132) = \{(1), (1 23), (132) \}\\ H(1 2) = H(1 3) = H(2 3) = \{ (1 2), (1 3), (2 3) \}\text{.} \end{gather*}
