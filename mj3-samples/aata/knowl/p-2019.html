Every finitely generated abelian group \(G\) is isomorphic to a direct product of cyclic groups of the form

\begin{equation*} {\mathbb Z}_{p_1^{ \alpha_1 }} \times {\mathbb Z}_{p_2^{ \alpha_2 }} \times \cdots \times {\mathbb Z}_{p_n^{ \alpha_n }} \times {\mathbb Z} \times \cdots \times {\mathbb Z}\text{,} \end{equation*}

where the \(p_i\)'s are primes (not necessarily distinct).
