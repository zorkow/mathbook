Lemma 18.3.

The set of equivalence classes of \(S\text{,}\) \(F_D\text{,}\) under the equivalence relation \(\sim\text{,}\) together with the operations of addition and multiplication defined by

\begin{align*} [a, b] + [c, d] & = [ad + b c, b d]\\ [ a, b] \cdot [c, d] & = [ac, b d]\text{,} \end{align*}

is a field.
