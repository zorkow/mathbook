(1) Observe that

\begin{align*} A \cup A & = \{ x : x \in A \text{ or } x \in A \}\\ & = \{ x : x \in A \}\\ & = A \end{align*}

and

\begin{align*} A \cap A & = \{ x : x \in A \text{ and } x \in A \}\\ & = \{ x : x \in A \}\\ & = A\text{.} \end{align*}

Also, \(A \setminus A = A \cap A' = \emptyset\text{.}\)
